## How Each Report Works

### Revenue Report
- Groups invoices by month
- Shows: invoiced amount, collected (paid), outstanding
- Table: individual invoice rows with all details
- Chart: stacked bar (collected stacked on top of outstanding)

### Receivables Aging
- Per-client aging buckets (current, 1-30, 31-60, 61-90, 90+ days)
- Table: client rows with age breakdown
- Only includes unpaid/partial invoices
- Color-coded buckets (green → yellow → orange → red)

### Client Revenue
- Per-client revenue breakdown
- Shows: invoices count, total invoiced, total paid, outstanding, % of revenue
- Sorted by revenue (descending)
- Flags revenue concentration (top client > 40%)

### Tax Report
- Income received ONLY (amount_paid, not invoiced)
- Groups by paid_date (not created_at)
- Shows: monthly income, paid invoice count, cumulative
- Chart: stacked bars for income, line for cumulative total
- For tax filing purposes

### Payment History
- Full payment log from payment_records table
- Shows: date, invoice #, client, amount, payment method, type (full/partial)
- No client filter (shows all payments)
- Sortable, paginated (last 100)
