## What is Prism?

An integrated ops platform for freelancers & small agencies. One place for:
- Contracts & proposals
- Client CRM
- Invoicing
- Payment settlement (USDC on Base)

## Why Now?

Freelancers use 5-7 tools (Word, Stripe, Notion, Google Sheets). Prism consolidates into one.

**Target audience:** Crypto-native builders, YC founders, indie hackers (Phase 1)  
**Payment:** USDC on Base (via Bankr), later Stripe  
**Pricing:** $49/mo free → $299/mo pro
