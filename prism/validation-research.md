# Prism Validation Research

## Goal

Find 10-15 freelancers/agency owners to interview. Validate:
1. Do they actually struggle with ops fragmentation?
2. Would they pay for a unified solution?
3. How much would they pay?
4. What features matter most?
5. Would crypto payment (USDC) be a dealbreaker or a feature?

## Target Audience

- Solopreneurs (1 person, $5k-50k MRR)
- Small agencies (2-5 people, $10k-100k MRR)
- Crypto/builder community (YC, Indie Hackers, crypto Twitter)
- Preferred: People who manage multiple clients + contracts

## Interview Questions (20-30 min)

1. **Pain:** "Walk me through how you manage client contracts, proposals, and invoicing today. What's frustrating?"
2. **Workflow:** "How many tools do you use? How do they talk to each other?"
3. **Willingness to pay:** "If a single platform solved this, what would you pay/month?"
4. **Crypto:** "Would you be open to payments in USDC (stablecoin on Base)? Would that be a feature or a problem?"
5. **Features:** "What's the #1 thing that would save you the most time?"

## Sources for Finding Interviewees

- [ ] Twitter (crypto/builder communities, #buildinpublic)
- [ ] Indie Hackers (freelancer threads)
- [ ] Reddit (r/freelance, r/agencygrowth, r/entrepreneur)
- [ ] Discord (crypto communities, builder DAOs)
- [ ] Cold outreach (DMs, email)
