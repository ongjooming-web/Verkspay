## Decision Tree: Stay vs Pivot

```
After 10-15 Validation Interviews:

Did we get strong signals?
├─ YES: Would 7+ out of 10 buy?
│   └─ Go to PHASE 2 (BUILD)
│
└─ NO: Would less than 5 out of 10 buy?
    └─ PIVOT: Try different vertical
        ├─ Option A: Different freelance angle (dev-only, design-only)
        ├─ Option B: Different vertical entirely (pick new one)
        └─ Option C: Hybrid model (service + product)
```

## Capital Burn vs Revenue

```
Weeks 1-4 (Validate + Build):
  Burn: $300 (validation) + ~$100 (infra) = $400
  Revenue: $0
  Runway: $600 remaining

Weeks 5-8 (Launch + Early Sales):
  Burn: ~$100 (hosting + tools)
  Revenue: $500-2K (first customers)
  Runway: Potentially cash-flow positive

Weeks 9-12 (Scale):
  Burn: ~$200 (marketing + growth)
  Revenue: $2K-5K (compounding)
  Runway: Sustainable, fund Phase 2 expansion

6+ months:
  Revenue: $20K-100K possible
  Burn: Self-funded from revenue
```
