## The Problem

Freelancers and agencies face three pain points that kill productivity:

1. **Tool Fragmentation**
   - Proposals: Google Docs or separate tools
   - Contracts: Manual templates or legal services
   - Invoicing: Stripe, Wave, or Excel
   - CRM: Airtable, Notion, or pen & paper
   - Payments: 2-3% fees, 2-5 day settlement
   - **Result:** 5-10 hours/week wasted switching between tools

2. **Slow Payments**
   - Traditional processors: 2-5 day settlement
   - International transfers: 7-14 days
   - Fees: 2.2-3.5% per transaction
   - **Result:** Cash flow pain, lost revenue to fees

3. **No Crypto-Native Tools**
   - Existing solutions ignore crypto payment adoption
   - Freelancers want USDC payments (instant, borderless)
   - No platforms bridge Web2 and Web3 seamlessly
   - **Result:** Underserved market with high willingness to pay

## The Solution: Prism

A single dashboard that consolidates operations and enables instant USDC settlements.

### Core Features (MVP)

**Proposals & Contracts**
- Beautiful, professional templates
- One-click proposal generation
- E-signature integration (Docusign, HelloSign)
- Smart contract templates for crypto work
- **Benefit:** Close deals faster, reduce admin overhead

**Smart CRM**
- Client database with contact history
- Proposal/invoice/project tracking per client
- Notes and relationship timelines
- Custom fields for service types
- **Benefit:** Understand revenue per client, repeat business

**Invoicing**
- Create, send, track invoices in seconds
- Automated reminders for overdue payments
- Revenue dashboard with real-time metrics
- Tax-ready reporting
- **Benefit:** Get paid faster, reduce chasing

**Instant Crypto Payments**
- Accept USDC on Base, Ethereum, Solana
- Real-time settlement (not 2-5 days)
- Wallet integration (MetaMask, WalletConnect)
- Low/no fees (vs 2.2% with Stripe)
- **Benefit:** Cash flow control, zero-fee international

**Dashboard & Analytics**
- Revenue by client/project/month
- Pipeline tracking (pending → paid)
- Overdue invoices alert
- Performance metrics per client
- **Benefit:** Make data-driven decisions
